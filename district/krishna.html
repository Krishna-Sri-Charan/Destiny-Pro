Krishna Attractions

Sri Durga nageshwara swamy temple
A historic temple near Tenali dedicated to Lord Shiva and Goddess Durga, famous for its carvings and cultural significance.
Cost to Stay: ₹1500 per night
Challapalli Rajavari Fort
A historic fort in Krishna District, symbolizing the legacy of the Challapalli Zamindars and their architectural grandeur.
Cost to Stay: ₹500 per night
Sri thirupathama temple
Dedicated to Goddess Tirupathamma at Penuganchiprolu, it is a revered site symbolizing maternal grace and divine blessings.
Cost to Stay: ₹500/night
prakasam barrage
A picturesque bridge on the Krishna River, connecting Kurnool and Krishna districts, and serving as a vital irrigation resource.
Cost to Stay: ₹2000/night
