Kannur Attractions

Kannur Lighthouse
An iconic lighthouse with scenic views of the Arabian Sea, a popular spot for tourists and locals.
Cost to Stay: ₹896
Payyambalam Beach
A serene beach known for its golden sands and clear waters, ideal for relaxation and picnics.
Cost to Stay: ₹1790
Arakkal Museum
A historic museum showcasing the heritage of the Arakkal family, the only Muslim royal family in Kerala.
Cost to Stay: ₹4829
