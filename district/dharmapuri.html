Dharmapuri Attractions

Kiliyur Falls
A beautiful waterfall located near the hills.
Cost to Stay: $50 - $100
Theerthamalai
A sacred place known for its temples. Lord Theerthagireeswar (Shiva) is the worshipping deity.
Cost to Stay: $30 - $70
Kottai Kovil
An ancient temple with intricate carvings.
Cost to Stay: $40 - $80
