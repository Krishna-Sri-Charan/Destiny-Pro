Nandyal Attractions

Belum caves
The second-largest caves in India, with fascinating stalactite and stalagmite formations.
Cost to Stay: 1500 per night
Yaganti temple
Known for the ancient Uma Maheswara Temple and a growing Nandi statue.
Cost to Stay: ₹500 per night
Ahobilam
A pilgrimage site with temples dedicated to Lord Narasimha.
Cost to Stay: ₹1000/night
Sri bramaramba temple
One of the twelve Jyotirlinga temples, located at Srisailam.
Cost to Stay: ₹3000 per night
