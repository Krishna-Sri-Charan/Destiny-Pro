Kurnool Attractions

Sri sakshi Ganapathi
A nearby temple where Lord Ganesha is believed to witness and record pilgrims' visits to Srisailam.
Cost to Stay: ₹1500 per night
Srisailam
A sacred town home to the revered Mallikarjuna Swamy and Brahmaramba temples, one of India's 12 Jyotirlingas.
Cost to Stay: ₹1500 per night
Mantralayam
Known as the resting place of Sri Raghavendra Swamy, this spiritual hub attracts devotees seeking his blessings and divine peace.
Cost to Stay: ₹3500/night
