Nellore Attractions

sri thalpagiri temple
Located in Chittoor District, this ancient temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is known for its peaceful ambiance and scenic surroundings.
Cost to Stay: ₹3500 per night
Mypadu beach
Situated in the Nellore District, this serene and less-crowded beach offers a calm retreat with its golden sands and picturesque views of the Bay of Bengal.
Cost to Stay: ₹2000 per night
