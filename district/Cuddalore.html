Cuddalore Attractions

Thiruvanthipuram Devanatha Svami Temple
A historic and spiritual landmark in Cuddalore, known for its serene atmosphere and religious significance. It attracts many pilgrims and visitors for its beautiful architecture and peaceful environment.
Cost to Stay: Sri Aralakam Restaurant
Silver Cascade Falls
A stunning waterfall in Cuddalore, Silver Cascade Falls is famous for its pristine beauty. Visitors come here to enjoy the tranquil surroundings and to experience the fresh, cool water. Perfect for photography and relaxation.
Cost to Stay: Al Baiq Family Restaurant
Kumbakkarai Falls
Kumbakkarai Falls is one of the most beautiful and popular waterfalls near Cuddalore. Known for its natural beauty and picnic spots, it is ideal for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts. The falls are a popular destination for weekend getaways.
Cost to Stay: AI Shafa Family Restaurant
