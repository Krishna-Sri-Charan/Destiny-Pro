Virudhunagar Attractions

Sanjeevi Hills
Sanjeevi Hills is known for its scenic beauty and serene environment. It is an excellent spot for trekking, nature walks, and capturing stunning panoramic views of the surrounding landscape. The place is rich in flora and fauna, making it ideal for nature enthusiasts.

Srivilliputhur Andal Temple
Srivilliputhur Andal Temple is a famous temple dedicated to the goddess Andal, located in the town of Srivilliputhur in Virudhunagar. It is an architectural marvel and a major pilgrimage site for Hindus. The temple's tall gopuram (tower) and vibrant festivals attract numerous visitors.

Ayyanar Falls
Ayyanar Falls is a beautiful waterfall located in the midst of lush greenery. It is a popular picnic spot, offering a peaceful retreat from the hustle and bustle of the city. The falls are surrounded by natural beauty, perfect for nature lovers and photographers.
