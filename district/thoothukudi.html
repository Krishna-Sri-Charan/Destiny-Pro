Thoothukudi Attractions

Thiruchendar Murugan Temple
A revered temple dedicated to Lord Murugan, known for its beautiful architecture and serene atmosphere.
Cost to Stay: ₹1,000 - ₹3,000 per night
Sri Vaikuntanathar Perumal Temple
An ancient temple featuring intricate carvings and a rich history, attracting pilgrims and tourists alike.
Cost to Stay: ₹800 - ₹2,500 per night
Kalugumalai
A scenic hill known for its breathtaking views and ancient rock-cut temples, ideal for hiking and photography.
Cost to Stay: ₹1,200 - ₹3,500 per night
