Madurai Attractions

Shri Amman Temple
A popular Hindu temple dedicated to Amman, located in the heart of Madurai. It's famous for its religious significance and historical architecture.
Cost to Stay: ₹2000 - ₹3000
Arulmigu Kallazhagar Thirukkoli
This is a sacred temple located on the outskirts of Madurai, known for its beautiful natural setting and serene atmosphere.
Cost to Stay: ₹1500 - ₹2500
Sri Meenakshi Sundaresvarar Temple
One of the most famous temples in India, known for its stunning Dravidian architecture and religious significance.
Cost to Stay: ₹3000 - ₹5000
Gandhi Memorial Museum
A museum dedicated to Mahatma Gandhi, showcasing his life and works. It is located in a historic building in Madurai.
Cost to Stay: ₹1200 - ₹2000
